FootRank Privacy Policy

Last updated: 9 June 2026

FootRank ("we", "us", "the app") is a 5-a-side football ranking and matchmaking application. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information we collect

  • Account information: your name, username, email address, and (if you choose) profile photo. When you sign in with Google, Apple, or Facebook, we receive your name, email, and profile picture from that provider to create your account.
  • Profile & gameplay data: preferred position, city, team membership, match results, ratings (Pitch Power), reliability and behavior scores.
  • Content you create: teams, match requests, scores, and behavior reports.

How we use your information

  • To create and manage your account and profile.
  • To match teams, schedule matches, and calculate rankings.
  • To send match-related notifications.
  • To keep the service fair and secure (e.g. dispute handling).

Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Your name, photo, position, city, team, and ratings are visible to other FootRank users as part of the app's social and ranking features. We use Supabase (database, authentication, storage) and Firebase (push notifications) as service providers to operate the app.

Data retention & deletion

We keep your data while your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below; we will remove your personal data within 30 days.

Security

Access to your data is protected with industry-standard authentication and row-level security. Sign-in is handled by trusted providers (Supabase Auth, Google, Apple, Facebook).

Children

FootRank is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their data.

Changes

We may update this policy; the "Last updated" date will change accordingly.
