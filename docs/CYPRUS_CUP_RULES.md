## The basics
- **What:** the team with the highest Pitch Power (PWR) at the end of the season
  tops the FootRank Cyprus Cup.
- **Where:** all of Cyprus (5-a-side / futsal).
- **When:** [Start date] 00:00 → [End date] 23:59 (a [4–6] week season).
- **Cost:** free to enter.
- **Prize:** [e.g. team kit set / free pitch hours / €X voucher] for the #1 team,
  plus [optional] "Top Player" award for the highest-PWR player with 5+ matches.

## How to enter
1. Download FootRank and create your profile.
2. Create your team (or join one with an invite code).
3. Play at least **[3]** confirmed matches during the season to qualify.

## How ranking works
- Win/lose confirmed matches; your team's **Pitch Power** rises or falls with each result.
- Beating higher-rated teams earns more; the table updates automatically.
- Final standings = PWR at the season deadline.

## Fair play
- Both captains confirm each match score in-app. Results must reflect real matches.
- Repeated score disputes can flag a captain and dock the team 500 PWR (built into
  the app) — so report honestly.
- Minimum **[3]** matches to be eligible for prizes (stops one-game flukes).
- Teams must be real; obvious fake teams/results are disqualified.
- FootRank's decision on disputes is final.

## Tie-breakers (if PWR is level)
1. Most confirmed matches played
2. Higher win count
3. Earlier to reach the final PWR

## Winners
- Announced within [3] days of the deadline on @footrank.cy.
- Winning captain contacted in-app / by the contact details on their profile.

## Good to know
- One team per captain.
- Organisers reserve the right to adjust rules for fairness and to extend dates if
  needed (announced on @footrank.cy).
