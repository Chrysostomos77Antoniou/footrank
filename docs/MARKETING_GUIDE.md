# FootRank — Step-by-Step Marketing Guide

A practical, do-this-then-that playbook for launching FootRank in Cyprus.
Context: solo founder + friends, ~€50–300/month, goal = users fast, market = all Cyprus
(effort concentrated on Nicosia + Limassol first for match density).

> The golden rule for a matchmaking app: **density beats reach.** A user who
> finds no opponent churns. Always make sure new teams can actually get a match.
> Health metric = **confirmed matches per week**, not just signups.

---

## PHASE 0 — Foundations (before you tell anyone) · ~3–5 days

1. **Brand basics**
   - App name: FootRank. Tagline: "Rank up. Find matches. Play."
   - Colours: lime `#C7F032` on navy `#14182B` (match the app).
   - Make a logo PNG + a simple "wordmark" for video intros/outros.
2. **Create the accounts** (same handle everywhere, e.g. `@footrank.cy`)
   - Instagram (primary), TikTok (primary), Facebook page (older players + groups),
     a public WhatsApp/“updates” channel.
   - Bio: "🇨🇾 Cyprus 5-a-side, ranked. Create a team, find matches, climb the table. ⬇️ Get the app"
   - Link: Play Store link (and a simple Linktree if you want privacy policy + app links together).
3. **Prepare 10–15 pieces of content** (so you're not scrambling at launch — see templates below).
4. **Set up a way to measure**
   - A simple spreadsheet: date, signups, teams created, confirmed matches, city.
   - Pull these weekly from your Supabase dashboard.

---

## PHASE 1 — Seed the market (manual, unglamorous, essential) · ~2 weeks

You must avoid launching to an empty app. Do this BEFORE any ads.

1. **List every 5-a-side group you can reach.** Your own WhatsApp/Viber groups,
   friends' groups, workplace teams, university teams. Aim for a list of 30+ organisers.
2. **Personally onboard 20–30 teams** in Nicosia + Limassol.
   - DM the organiser (script below). Help them create the team + invite players.
   - Ask each to create one open match request.
3. **Stage a few real matches** so the first public users see live activity:
   confirmed matches on the board, populated rankings.
4. **Recruit 3–5 "champions"** — enthusiastic organisers who'll spread it in their
   circles. Give them status (e.g., featured team, early-access badge, free Cup entry).

**Organiser DM script (copy/paste):**
> "Yo [name] 👋 I built a free app, FootRank — it's like a league table for our
> 5-a-side. You create your team, find other teams to play, log the score, and
> climb a Cyprus-wide ranking. I'm starting with a few serious teams — want yours
> to be one of the first on the board? Takes 2 mins, I'll set it up with you."

---

## PHASE 2 — Launch with a competition (the growth spike) · Week 1

The best "users fast" lever for a ranking app is a **time-boxed competition**.

1. **Announce the "FootRank Cyprus Cup"**
   - 4–6 week season. Teams climb the Pitch Power leaderboard by playing & winning.
   - Prize for #1 team (and maybe top player). See sponsor section to make it free.
   - Clear start/end date → urgency → sign up NOW.
2. **Launch day content blitz** (you + friends post same day):
   - Announcement reel ("Cyprus, your 5-a-side now has a table 🏆").
   - Story countdown, "first 20 teams get X".
   - Everyone shares to their groups simultaneously.
3. **Seed teams compete day 1** so the Cup looks alive immediately.
4. **Post the leaderboard daily** as a story ("Current top 5 in Cyprus 👀").
   Rivalry + FOMO = the engine.

## PHASE 3 — Sustain & scale · Weeks 2–8

### Content (your main free channel — 4–6 posts/week)
- **Match clips → result card → leaderboard climb** (most important format).
- **Rivalries / call-outs**: "Team A says they'd beat Team B. Settle it on FootRank."
- **Top of the table**: weekly "Team of the Week", biggest PWR gain, upset of the week.
- **How-to**: 15-sec "how to create your team" / "how to find a match".
- **UGC**: repost teams' own clips; ask them to tag @footrank.cy.
- Post to IG Reels + TikTok + Facebook. Same clip, all platforms.

### Partnerships (free distribution)
- **5-a-side / futsal venues**: put a QR poster at the pitch ("Rank tonight's match").
  Offer them a shout-out / "Venues" feature in return.
- **Universities**: intramural organisers, football societies (UCY, Frederick, UCLan Cy).
- **Sportswear / football shops**: cross-promo, prize sponsorship.

### Micro-influencers (use your small budget here)
- 2–3 Cypriot football pages / semi-pro players (10–50k followers).
- Pay small or give free Cup entry / featured status. One authentic shout-out from a
  local football page > a generic ad.

### Paid ads (only once a city has liquidity)
- IG/TikTok, geo = Cyprus, men 18–35, interests: football, futsal, 5-a-side.
- Start €5–10/day boosting your **best-performing organic reel** (don't make ad-only creative).
- Send to the Play Store listing. Watch cost-per-install; kill losers fast.

## Built-in virality (turn the product into a growth loop)
- Every confirmed match needs an opponent → each active team recruits another.
- Push the **invite code** in content ("share your code, build your squad").
- Idea for later: reward the player/team who refers the most new teams (free Cup entry,
  featured badge). Ask the dev (me) to add a referral counter if you want this tracked.

---

## Budget allocation (€50–300/month)
| Item | Share | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Micro-influencer shout-outs | 40% | Best ROI for trust + reach in a small market |
| Paid social boosts | 30% | Only after density; boost proven reels |
| Prize / content props | 20% | Or get sponsored (ideal) |
| Tools (Canva Pro, scheduling) | 10% | Optional; free tiers work at first |

Friends doing content at a discount → spend the savings on **more posts + more
micro-influencers**, not on fancy production.

## Sponsor pitch (send to pitch owners / shops)
> "Hi [venue/shop] — I run FootRank, a fast-growing app where Cyprus 5-a-side
> teams rank and find matches. I'm launching the FootRank Cyprus Cup with
> [X] teams already signed up. I'm looking for one prize sponsor. In return you'd
> get: your logo on all Cup posts, a shout-out to every team in the app, and
> 'official venue/partner' status. Prize could be as simple as [free pitch hours /
> a kit voucher]. Interested in a 10-min chat?"

## 8-week launch calendar (at a glance)
- **Wk -1 (Phase 0):** accounts, branding, 15 content pieces ready, tracking sheet.
- **Wk 1–2 (Phase 1):** seed 20–30 teams, stage matches, recruit champions, line up a sponsor.
- **Wk 3 (Phase 2):** announce + launch the Cup, content blitz, daily leaderboard stories.
- **Wk 4–7 (Phase 3):** 4–6 posts/wk, venue QR posters, 2–3 influencer shout-outs, small paid boosts.
- **Wk 8:** Cup final + winner announcement (huge content moment) → roll straight into Season 2.

---

## Weekly routine (repeat)
- **Mon:** post Team of the Week + table update; plan the week's clips.
- **Tue–Thu:** 1 reel/day (match clips, rivalries, how-to).
- **Fri:** "fixtures this weekend" + hype.
- **Sun:** collect match clips from teams; check the metrics sheet.

---

## What "good" looks like (targets to aim for)
- 50%+ of new teams get a match within 7 days (liquidity is healthy).
- Confirmed matches/week trending up every week.
- A growing, *active* leaderboard in Nicosia & Limassol before pushing other cities.

---

## Common mistakes to avoid
- ❌ Paying for ads before the app has live matches → wasted money, churn.
- ❌ Spreading thin across all cities equally → no density anywhere.
- ❌ Chasing vanity signups → track **matches**, the real value.
- ❌ One-off launch with no follow-up → momentum dies; run **seasons** to keep it alive.
