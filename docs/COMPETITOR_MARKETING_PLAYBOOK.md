## PART 1 — What similar companies did (and what to copy)

### Playtomic (padel matchmaking — closest model to you)
- **Level-based matchmaking as the hero message** — "find players at your level."
  *Copy this:* lead with "same city, same level, ready to play," not features.
- **Club/venue partnerships were the growth engine** — integrated with courts.
  *Copy:* partner with 5-a-side pitch operators; be where matches already happen.
- **App-first, community-led**, minimal early paid spend.
*Takeaway: own the venues + level-matching narrative.*

### Strava (ranking, segments, social — closest to your *mechanic*)
- **Leaderboards + segments = built-in bragging rights and sharing.**
  *Copy:* your Pitch Power table IS the product's viral hook — make results shareable.
- **Challenges** (monthly) drive re-engagement spikes. *Copy:* run seasons / the Cyprus Cup.
- **Auto-generated shareable cards** after each activity. *Copy:* the WON/LOST card is your
  shareable unit — encourage screenshots to stories.
*Takeaway: competition + shareable result cards + recurring challenges.*

### Plei / Footy Addicts / Good Rec (pickup football/sports)
- **Organiser-led growth** — recruit the people who already run games; they bring 10–20 each.
  *Copy:* your Phase-1 seeding of 20–30 organisers is exactly this.
- **Heavy IG/TikTok of real games** — raw match clips, not polished ads.
- **Density-per-area focus** — one neighbourhood/city at a time.
*Takeaway: organisers are your distribution; raw clips beat ads.*

### Spond / TeamSnap / TeamUp (team management)
- **Word-of-mouth through the organiser** — one captain onboards a whole team for free.
  *Copy:* the invite code does this; push "invite your squad."
- **Freemium / free core** removed signup friction. *Copy:* keep core free.
*Takeaway: one captain = a whole team of installs. Optimise for captain referrals.*

### Gymshark / modern sports DTC (community + influencers)
- **Built community via micro-influencers + ambassadors**, not big ads.
  *Copy:* recruit local footballers/teams as "FootRank ambassadors."
- **Reposting user content** built loyalty cheaply. *Copy:* repost every team clip.
*Takeaway: ambassadors + UGC > ad budget for a small brand.*

### Duolingo / mobile-app TikTok playbook (organic reach)
- **A bold, funny brand personality on TikTok** drove millions of organic views at ~€0.
  *Copy:* give FootRank banter/rivalry personality; lean into football culture humour.
*Takeaway: personality is free reach.*

### The common pattern across all of them
1. **Pick a niche + area and dominate it** (density).
2. **Recruit the connectors** (organisers/captains) — they bring the crowd.
3. **Make the product self-promote** (shareable result cards, leaderboards, invite codes).
4. **Run recurring challenges/seasons** for repeat spikes.
5. **Organic content + micro-influencers first; paid only to amplify what already works.**

## PART 2 — Platform-by-platform playbook

### Instagram (primary — discovery + credibility)
- **Format priority:** Reels > Stories > carousel posts > static.
- **Reels (3–5/week):** match clip → WON card → leaderboard climb; rivalry call-outs;
  "team of the week." 7–15 sec, hook in first 1 sec, trending audio, captions on-screen.
- **Stories (daily during Cup):** leaderboard top-5, polls ("who wins this weekend?"),
  countdowns, repost team tags. Use the **Link sticker** to the Play Store.
- **Profile:** clear bio + CTA, story highlights ("How it works", "The Cup", "Teams").
- **Growth tactics:** reply to every comment fast (boosts ranking), use 3–5 relevant
  hashtags (not 30), collab posts (`Collab` feature) with teams/venues to hit both audiences.
- **Avoid:** over-polished ads; IG punishes obvious TikTok watermarks — re-export clean.

### TikTok (primary — raw reach, youngest audience)
- **Most important platform for cheap reach.** Post 1/day if you can.
- **Style:** raw, fast, native. Football bangers, rivalries, "POV: your team is bottom of
  the table," reaction clips, "rating Cyprus 5-a-side teams."
- **Hook in 1 second** (motion or a bold claim) or people scroll.
- **Hashtags:** mix broad (#football #futsal) + niche (#cyprus #5aside) + 1 trend.
- **Trends:** jump on trending sounds within 2–3 days; football + trend = reach.
- **Series:** "Ranking every team in Nicosia," "Match of the week" — series build followers.
- **Reply to comments with a video** (huge reach multiplier on TikTok).

### Facebook (secondary — older players, groups, events)
- **The real value is GROUPS.** Join/participate in Cyprus football, 5-a-side, futsal,
  expat sports groups. Be helpful first; share results/clips, not spam.
- **Create a FootRank page** + post the same Reels (cross-post from IG).
- **Facebook Events** for the Cup — invite, RSVP, reminders.
- **Marketplace/Local** isn't relevant; focus on Groups + Events.

### WhatsApp / Viber (the Cyprus growth engine — don't skip)
- This is where 5-a-side is actually organised locally.
- **Broadcast/channel** for results, fixtures, leaderboard updates.
- **Seed via organiser DMs** (script in MARKETING_GUIDE). Personal > public here.
- Add a "share to WhatsApp" habit: post the leaderboard image weekly for organisers to forward.

### YouTube Shorts (free extra reach — repurpose)
- Re-upload your TikToks/Reels as Shorts. Zero extra work, extra discovery.
- Optional: a longer "Cup highlights" video at season end.

### Google Play Store (ASO — free installs from search)
- **Title/short description** with keywords: football, 5-a-side, matchmaking, ranking, league.
- **First 2 screenshots** carry the listing — use your best (Teams ranking, Matches).
- **Ask happy users for ratings** in-app after a confirmed match (boosts ranking).
- Localise the listing in **Greek** for Cyprus.
- (See `STORE_LISTING.md` for the full copy.)

### Google / Search (light)
- A one-page site (or your GitHub Pages) with the app links + "5-a-side Cyprus" keywords.
- Optional later: a few €/day Google App campaign once you have budget.

## PART 3 — Growth loops to build in (beyond posting)

1. **Result-card loop:** every match → shareable WON card → screenshot to story → friends see → install.
2. **Captain loop:** one captain installs → invites 5–10 players via code.
3. **Opponent loop:** to play, you need an opponent → teams recruit teams.
4. **Season loop:** Cup ends → winners posted → Season 2 starts → re-engagement.
5. **Ambassador loop:** recruit ~10 team captains as ambassadors; they post + recruit for status/perks.

> Ask the dev (me) to add a **referral counter** and a **one-tap "share my result card"**
> if you want loops #1 and #2 tracked and frictionless — both are high-impact, low-effort builds.

## PART 4 — Influencers & ambassadors (your paid budget's best use)
- **Tiers:** nano (1–10k) and micro (10–50k) Cypriot football pages/players convert best.
- **Offer:** small fee OR free Cup entry / "official ambassador" status / featured team.
- **Brief them lightly** — authentic > scripted. Must disclose (#ad / paid partnership).
- **2–3 well-chosen local shout-outs** beat one generic ad in a market this size.

---

## PART 5 — Paid ads (only after a city has live matches)
- **Where:** TikTok + Instagram (Meta). Start tiny (€5–10/day).
- **What:** boost your best-performing ORGANIC reel (proven hook) — don't make ad-only creative.
- **Targeting:** Cyprus, men 18–35, interests football/futsal.
- **Goal:** app installs; watch cost-per-install and (more importantly) whether installs
  become **confirmed matches**. Kill anything that installs but doesn't activate.

---

## PART 6 — KPIs (what to track weekly)
- Confirmed matches / week ← the real health metric
- New teams, % matched within 7 days (liquidity)
- Signups by city; Week-1 retention
- Per channel: views → profile visits → installs (find your best channel, double down)
- Cost per install AND cost per first-match (paid)

---

## PART 7 — 90-day timeline
- **Days 1–14:** accounts + 15 content pieces; seed 20–30 teams; line up sponsor + 3 ambassadors.
- **Days 15–21:** launch + Cyprus Cup announcement; content blitz; daily stories.
- **Days 22–60:** 1 TikTok/day, 4–5 Reels/wk, venue QRs, 2–3 influencer posts, small boosts;
  weekly leaderboard + Team of the Week.
- **Days 61–75:** Cup final + winners (big content); collect testimonials/clips.
- **Days 76–90:** launch Season 2; expand effort to the next city; start light ASO/Google.

---

## The one-line strategy
**Dominate Nicosia + Limassol by recruiting organisers, make every result shareable,
run recurring Cup seasons, and let TikTok/Reels + local ambassadors do the reach — pay
only to amplify what's already working.**
