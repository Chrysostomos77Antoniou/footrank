# FootRank — Screenshots & Store Assets Plan

A practical shot list + specs so you can produce store screenshots quickly.

## How to capture (on your phone, release build)
1. Use a clean account with some data (a team, a few confirmed/completed matches).
2. Put the phone in **light mode** (cleaner for store listings) — capture a few
   in **dark mode** too if you want to show it off.
3. Take screenshots with the phone (Power + Volume Down). They'll be the right
   native resolution for your device; Play accepts phone-native sizes.

## The 6 shots (in order — first 2 matter most)
1. **Home / Hero** — "Climb the ranks" banner + quick actions. Caption: *"Your 5-a-side, ranked."*
2. **Rankings (Players)** — leaderboard with PWR + medals. Caption: *"Climb the Pitch Power leaderboard."*
3. **Matches** — Available Opponents + Upcoming + a WON badge in history. Caption: *"Find opponents. Play. Log results."*
4. **Match detail** — score + Captains contact card. Caption: *"Fair results, confirmed by both captains."*
5. **Team page** — logo, record (W-L), squad. Caption: *"Build your squad."*
6. **Profile** — stats (PWR, reliability, behavior) + match history. Caption: *"Track your game."*

> Optional 7th: the Login screen with the video background + social buttons.

## Adding captions / frames (optional but recommended)
- Free tools: **screenshots.pro**, **AppMockUp**, or Figma.
- Put each raw screenshot inside a phone frame on a solid brand background
  (lime `#C7F032` on navy `#14182B`, matching the app) with a one-line caption
  at the top.

## Required sizes
### Google Play (minimum)
- **Phone screenshots**: 2–8 images, 16:9 or 9:16, each side 320–3840 px.
  (Your phone's native portrait screenshots are fine.)
- **Feature graphic**: 1024 × 500 PNG/JPG (no transparency). One hero image —
  logo + tagline "Rank up. Find matches. Play." on the brand background.
- **App icon**: 512 × 512 (already have it).

### Apple App Store (only if you ship iOS)
- **6.7"**: 1290 × 2796 (3–10 shots)
- **6.5"**: 1242 × 2688
- **App icon**: 1024 × 1024 (no alpha)
